# Anthropic

**Anthropic** is the AI company that originated the [[mcp-specification|Model Context Protocol]] and develops the Claude family of models. MCP, including its authorization profile ([[mcp-authorization]]), is published as an open specification at <https://modelcontextprotocol.io> and developed in the open (`modelcontextprotocol` GitHub organization, including the [[mcp-authorization-extensions|ext-auth]] repository).

Per the confidence rules in this wiki, Anthropic engineering blog posts rank as **medium**-confidence sources; the MCP specification itself ranks as **high**.

## Relevance to this wiki

As steward of MCP, Anthropic is the source-of-record for the protocol that anchors much of this wiki's current coverage. Future ingests may add Anthropic engineering posts on agent identity, tool-use authorization, and related topics.
