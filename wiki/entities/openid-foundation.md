# OpenID Foundation

The **OpenID Foundation (OIDF)** is the standards body responsible for OpenID Connect (the identity layer on top of OAuth 2.0) and related specifications such as FAPI and AuthZEN.

## Outputs referenced by MCP Authorization

The [[mcp-authorization-overview]] relies on two OpenID Foundation specifications:

- [[openid-connect-discovery|OpenID Connect Discovery 1.0]] — one of the two mandatory [[authorization-server-discovery|AS discovery]] mechanisms.
- [[openid-connect-dynamic-client-registration|OpenID Connect Dynamic Client Registration 1.0]] — part of the (deprecated) Dynamic Client Registration path of [[client-registration]].

OpenID Foundation specs and drafts are **high**-confidence sources in this wiki.
